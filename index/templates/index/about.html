About Us

At Krumb, we are an artisan bakery, we focus on handmade, delicious fresh bakes. Our Cakes, Bakes & Buns are made using quality, local produces so we in turn can support other small producers and growers.

Our dream

Our dream is simple, we want to share home made Irish produce with everyone, and what better medium than cake??

What we offer?

We offer a Custom Cakes, desserts, tray bakes and treats for any occasion or celebration, You can order these via Email on hello@krumbbakery.com

Where can you see us?

We are found at local Farmers markets and events to keep up to date follow us on Instagram , @krumb_bakery and on Facebook to stay up to date on where you can find us next.
